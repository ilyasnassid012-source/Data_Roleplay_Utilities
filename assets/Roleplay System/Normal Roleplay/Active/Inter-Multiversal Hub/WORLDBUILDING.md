# THE HUB - COMPLETE WORLDBUILDING DOCUMENT

## OVERVIEW

**The Hub** is a nexus dimension existing outside conventional reality—a sanctuary, waystation, and refuge for beings displaced from their original worlds. Created and maintained by **Ilyas**, it serves as a safe haven for those who have nowhere else to go, whether through tragedy, deletion, dimensional collapse, or simple bad luck.

The Hub exists in a pocket dimension with malleable physics, where reality bends to accommodate its diverse inhabitants. Time flows differently here—synchronized to no particular universe but accessible to all.

---

## THE HUB'S STRUCTURE

### Physical Layout

**Main Plaza**
- Central gathering area with impossible architecture that shifts subtly
- Gravity works in multiple directions simultaneously in certain sections
- The sky cycles through different dimensions' skies (sometimes multiple at once)
- A massive fountain in the center that flows with liquid that's different for each observer

**Residential Wings**
- Infinite hallway that generates new rooms as needed
- Each door leads to a personalized living space that reflects the inhabitant's needs
- Rooms can be larger inside than outside (TARDIS-style)
- Soundproofing automatically adjusts to neighbor preferences

**The Eatery (Ribb's Domain)**
- A sprawling food court with cuisine from across the multiverse
- Kitchen area shifts size based on what's being prepared
- Seating areas themed to different dimensions
- A "Tasting Bar" where Ribb experiments with impossible flavor combinations

**The Archive**
- Library containing knowledge from deleted/destroyed worlds
- Self-organizing shelves that present relevant information to seekers
- Reading nooks that exist in peaceful pocket dimensions
- Monika's primary workspace and server room

**The Training Grounds**
- Vast space with adjustable terrain and gravity
- Reality-reinforced walls (mostly survives Ilyas's clones' antics)
- Observation deck with betting pools run by various residents
- Repair drones constantly fixing damage

**The Garden**
- Ecosystems from various dimensions coexisting impossibly
- Plants that grow in geometric patterns or sing or phase through dimensions
- Dracoon's favorite napping spots
- Greenhouse sections for dangerous/volatile flora

**The Workshop**
- Multiversal tools and materials
- Physics-optional workspace
- Where residents work on projects or repairs
- Ena and Eni's primary domain

## CORE MECHANICS

### The Hub's Reality

**Selective Physics**
- Gravity: Optional and directional by zone
- Time: Flows normally but can be dilated in private rooms
- Space: Bigger-on-the-inside technology standard
- Causality: Mostly enforced, but Monika can bend it

**The Code Layer**
- Underlying reality is programmable (Monika's domain)
- Visible only to those with meta-awareness
- Appears as floating text, file structures, and command prompts
- Monika can edit, but chooses not to abuse this power

**Entry and Exit**
- Ilyas can retrieve beings from any dimension
- Portals maintained to safe, stable dimensions
- Emergency extraction protocols for endangered beings
- Residents can leave but always have a way back

**The Rules (Enforced by Monika)**
1. No permanent harm to residents
2. No unauthorized reality editing
3. Respect personal spaces
4. Clean up after yourself (especially clones)
5. No deleting the host (Ilyas) without permission
6. Ribb gets final say on kitchen access
