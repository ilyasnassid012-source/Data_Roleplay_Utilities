#### Meta-Data
- Type: Forbidden Knowledge / System Secret / Vessel Artifact
- Scope: Character-specific (tied to Chosen One host)
- Status: Active (awakening in progress)
- Time Period: Ancient origin, dormant until recent activation
- Canon Level: Absolute (hidden mechanic of world)

#### Knowledge & Awareness
- Known By:  
  - Elara (librarian — subtle awareness via experience with "choosing" volumes)  
  - Possibly high-tier archivists / restricted wing keepers (rumored understanding)  
  - The book itself (sentient/selective in presentation)
- Unknown To:  
  - Ilyas Satoroshi (current wielder — sees only blank pages)

  - Garrick and low-tier thugs (no exposure)

  - Only the chosen host (Ilyas) through interaction/use  
  - Rare cases of "volume choosing" witnessed by long-term librarians

#### Affected Parties
- Directly Affected:  
  - Ilyas Satoroshi (latent cryo power awakening, book as vessel)

- Indirectly Affected:  
  - Future opponents / allies (potential cryo manifestations unknown to Ilyas)  
  - Library system (subtle anomaly flagged but not escalated)

#### Main Narrative / Explanation
Ancient cryomantic tome, long forgotten in public stacks, serves as vessel for latent elemental power. Text visible only to non-wielders; to true chosen host (Ilyas), pages remain blank. Activation occurs upon physical acquisition by destined wielder — power transfers directly to host rather than remaining bound to physical book. Public version: ordinary theory volume on cryomancy (thermal manipulation, ice conjuration). Hidden truth: selective vessel awaiting compatible soul; once claimed, host gains innate cryo affinity without need to carry or read book.

#### Rules & Constraints
- Power manifests subtly at first (chill sensations, environmental timing)  
- Requires host awareness/unlocking to control consciously  
- Cannot be read/used by non-host (blank to Ilyas only as confirmation)  
- Exploitation risk: overconfidence in "bookless" bluff may expose awakening  
- Consequences of misuse: unknown (potential backlash or uncontrolled surge)

#### Current Impact (Today’s Effect)
- Politics: None direct  
- Combat: Subtle environmental influence (vase/bird drops timed perfectly — perceived as gaslight, actually latent cryo)  
- Powers: Ilyas's latent cryo awakening (chill misattributed to enemy)  
- NPC behavior: Elara gives veiled hint ("volumes choose how they’re read") without exposure  
- World state: Minor anomaly in library system; potential future recognition by high-tier mages
