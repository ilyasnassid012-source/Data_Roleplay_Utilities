# Act 1 Chapter 0 — The Last Birthday

## DOCUMENTATION METADATA
- **Act Number:** 1
- **Chapter Number:** 0
- **Session Date(s):** January 13, 2026
- **Total Word Count:** ~18,000
- **Primary Characters:** Ilyas Sokawa, Sayuri Takahashi, Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa
- **New Characters Introduced:** Sayuri Takahashi, Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa, Ilyas's unnamed sister (flashback only)
- **Major Locations:** Tenketsu Academy (hallways, Class 1-C), Sokawa residence
- **Time Span:** Several hours on Ilyas's final birthday + several weeks epilogue
- **Genre/Tone Tags:** #Horror #Tragedy #Sacrifice #Isolation #Curse #Redemption

## TIMELINE — COMPLETE NARRATIVE RECONSTRUCTION

### MAIN FLOW: FULL CHRONOLOGICAL NARRATIVE

**OPENING CONTEXT:**  
Pre-B.T.M. timeline. Ilyas Sokawa attends Tenketsu Academy under constant social isolation due to rumors of a curse tied to his family tragedies. His curse manifests as lethal misfortune to those closest to him on his birthday. He avoids connection to protect others. Sayuri Takahashi is the only person who consistently approaches him despite the rumors, viewing it as bad luck rather than curse. Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa serves as vessel for the primary curse entity.

---

#### ***MAJOR SCENE 1: Morning Hallway Approach***
*Location:* Tenketsu Academy main hallway
*Time:* Early dawn, before first bell
*POV Character(s):* Environmental / Ilyas external
*Characters Present:* Ilyas Sokawa, Sayuri Takahashi, background students
*Scene Type:* **MAJOR NARRATIVE SCENE**

The hallway is cold and gray. Students part around Ilyas like water around stone. Whispers follow him—some mocking, some fearful. His shadow lags half a second behind his steps.

Sayuri waits near the shoe lockers, student council armband visible. She matches his pace from one corridor away, never closing the distance fully.

Sayuri Takahashi: "Morning, Sokawa."  
Context: Voice soft, carrying just far enough. Eyes forward. First acknowledgment in days.

Sayuri Takahashi: "You changed your route again. Third time this week."  
Context: Gentle observation, not accusation.

Sayuri Takahashi: "It's okay if you don't want to talk today. I just... wanted to say it before the bell."  
Context: Fingers brush armband—habit when serious.

Sayuri Takahashi: "Happy early birthday. Even if you pretend it isn't coming."

Ilyas offers only a single small nod—no words, no pause—then turns left toward classrooms. Sayuri allows the gap to widen naturally, heading toward the stairwell.

[**Hidden Layer:** The curse observes through shadows, noting proximity to Sayuri as potential "gift" target.]

[**Knowledge Status:**  
- Ilyas knows: Sayuri remembered his birthday despite his avoidance.  
- Sayuri knows: Ilyas is actively avoiding connection as birthday nears.  
- Audience knows: Birthday triggers lethal curse event on closest person.]

#### ***MAJOR SCENE 2: Classroom Curse Signs***
*Location:* Class 1-C
*Time:* Homeroom period
*POV Character(s):* Environmental / Ilyas external
*Characters Present:* Ilyas Sokawa, Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa, background students
*Scene Type:* **MAJOR NARRATIVE SCENE**

Ilyas enters Class 1-C. His desk has been thrown out the third-floor window—lies broken in courtyard below. He retrieves a spare chair, sits without desk.

Mr. Hasegawa notices immediately but comments only obliquely.

Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa: “Sokawa-kun. It appears your desk has... relocated.”  
Context: Half-smile widens slightly. Voice soft, carrying.

Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa: “A rather dramatic statement, would you not agree?”  
Context: Steps forward silently.

Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa: “Perhaps someone felt your studies would benefit from... closer proximity to the earth. Or perhaps it simply grew tired of waiting.”  
Context: Smile remains; eyes do not.

Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa: “You may remain as you are. For today.”

Ilyas attempts to write in notebook—pens dry, pencil tears page, then snaps in grip during triggered memory.

During attendance, Ilyas remains silent when name called. Marked absent despite physical presence.

[**Hidden Layer:** Curse actively sabotages materials; crow watches from window sill.]

[**Knowledge Status:**  
- Class knows: Ilyas marked absent despite being present.  
- Mr. Hasegawa knows: Ilyas's refusal to respond is deliberate.  
- Ilyas knows: Silence protects others from official record of closeness.]

---

#### ***MAJOR SCENE 3: Private Confrontation & Curse Awakening***
*Location:* Class 1-C (empty)
*Time:* Immediately after second bell
*POV Character(s):* Sayuri Takahashi
*Characters Present:* Sayuri Takahashi, Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa (curse vessel)
*Scene Type:* **MAJOR NARRATIVE SCENE**

Students exit quickly. Sayuri remains seated. Mr. Hasegawa requests private discussion.

Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa: “Takahashi-san. A moment, if you would be so kind.”  
Context: Voice low, polite but commanding.

Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa: “You have been... attentive to Sokawa-kun. It is admirable. Truly.”  
Context: Eyes too wide, unblinking.

Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa: “But attention has consequences. Especially when one insists on standing so close... to something that devours proximity.”  
Context: Pen points downward like needle.

Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa: “Some gifts... are best left unopened.”

Curse manifests visibly to Sayuri for first time—shadow hands emerge from all corners, encircling her slowly. Cold intensifies. She awakens to seeing curses fully. Recognizes teacher as inhuman vessel.

[**Hidden Layer:** Curse preparing lethal "birthday gift" targeting Sayuri as closest remaining person.]

[**Knowledge Status:**  
- Sayuri now knows: Curses are real; teacher is vessel; Ilyas likely sees this daily.  
- Mr. Hasegawa knows: Sayuri has awakened to curse sight.  
- Audience knows: Gift targets closest person on birthday.]

#### ***MAJOR SCENE 4: Curse Reversal & Sacrifice Declaration***
*Location:* Class 1-C
*Time:* Moments after awakening
*POV Character(s):* Environmental
*Characters Present:* Ilyas Sokawa, Sayuri Takahashi, Mr. Kazuo Hasegawa (vessel)
*Scene Type:* **MAJOR NARRATIVE SCENE**

Ilyas bursts in, throws sharpened pencil into teacher's shoulder. Curse hands hesitate.

Ilyas Sokawa: "You and others.. Only wait when i am gone to Go through it everyday."  
Context: Voice muffled through hand over mouth.

Ilyas Sokawa: "all those years, Its Time to End It with The Source."  
Context: Points at curse vessel.

Ilyas Sokawa: "you seek what closest. She is Indeed the Closest."  
Context: Calm, faint paranoid smile.

Ilyas Sokawa: "But you..., You are More closer, Not As a Friend or an Foe. The real Definition of Closest, By all Means."  
Context: Emotional reversal triggers curse hands to turn inward on vessel.

Curse collapses inward on Mr. Hasegawa—body folds, tendrils consume vessel. Husk remains. Sayuri released unharmed.

Ilyas bleeding from mouth, declares intent to leave forever.

Ilyas Sokawa: "I will go... Forever. Dont follow or try to. And let your Survival go to waste..."

Sayuri responds with acknowledgment of his return and sacrifice.

Ilyas final words before exit:

Ilyas Sokawa: "It doesn't Matter. all that mattered is I didn't get you killed too..."  
Context: Serene tone.

Ilyas Sokawa: "Also..., Thanks for remembering my birthday, Its the best birthday i ever had."

Students arrive, discover aftermath.

[**Knowledge Status:**  
- Sayuri knows: Ilyas deliberately reversed curse to save her, plans permanent departure.  
- Students know: Teacher collapsed/dead, classroom damaged, Sayuri present.  
- Audience knows: Ilyas intends self-sacrifice as final solution.]

#### ***MAJOR SCENE 5: Dual Timeline Sacrifice***
*Location:* Sokawa residence (both timelines)
*Time:* Late morning (present) / Late afternoon (flashback)
*POV Character(s):* Environmental
*Characters Present:* Ilyas's sister (flashback), Ilyas Sokawa (present)
*Scene Type:* **MEMORY & MAJOR NARRATIVE SCENE**

Flashback (13th birthday): Sister, consumed by curse-induced guilt, prepares cake then hangs herself from ceiling fan. Body sways, blood drips onto cake. Blank smile—believed she ended suffering.

Present: Ilyas returns home, prepares same room identically. Hangs himself deliberately. Knowing smile—sacrificial release of all curses. Shadows dissolve completely.

Timelines converge: both bodies sway. Sister's smile empty (guilt's lie). Ilyas's smile serene (choice's truth). Curses unravel permanently.

[**Knowledge Status:**  
- Audience knows: Ilyas's death mirrors sister's but with opposite intent—sacrifice vs. deception.  
- No in-universe character knows Ilyas's fate.]

---

#### ***FINAL SCENE 6: Weeks Later - Normalcy Returns***
*Location:* Tenketsu Academy various
*Time:* Several weeks post-sacrifice
*POV Character(s):* Sayuri Takahashi
*Characters Present:* Sayuri Takahashi, background students, new teacher Ms. Nakamura
*Scene Type:* **CHAPTER CONCLUSION**

School functions normally. New teacher. Empty chair untouched. Ilyas officially "transferred." House considered haunted—unchecked.

Sayuri continues routine but carries quiet weight. Sees faint residual curse wisps—non-threatening, dissolving. Reflects on Ilyas's final words and actions. Accepts his sacrifice gave others (including her) normal life.

Final image: Sayuri at window overlooking courtyard—grass green, no desk remnants. Palm to glass where red smear once was. Turns away, joins flow of ordinary school life.

[**Knowledge Status at Chapter End:**  
- Sayuri suspects: Ilyas ended himself to break curse cycle.  
- Everyone else believes: Ilyas simply transferred/disappeared.  
- Audience knows: Ilyas's sacrifice succeeded—curses gone permanently.

*Location: The White Void*
*Time: Immediate arrival*
*POV Character(s): Ilyas's Perspective*
*Characters Present: Ilyas Sokawa, Lyra*
*Scene Type: **MAJOR NARRATIVE SCENE***

LYRA: "Ooh~ Fresh meat! And pink-haired too—matching set!"

Lyra enthusiastic, breaks down options: Land of Waves, Academy, Chunin Exams, custom blend. Confirms age-down to 12-14. Chat going feral.

Ilyas specifies no befriending, only throwing hands and gaslights. New mission in village, neutral wanderer, learn jutsu with low chakra, antagonists Orochimaru loyalist group.

Lyra excited, builds scenario: early Part 1, C-rank mission village, pink-haired kid local, Orochimaru splinter cell kidnapping target. Small chakra pool, basic jutsu seed. Age-down to 13. Shows mission preview card.

Ilyas materializes in Orenji-mura as 13-year-old, pink ponytail, local attire, hidden weights and Key. Walks, notices small legs. Haruka recognizes him, gives orange bread. Notices treeline watchers with snake tattoo.
