## Lore

### Essence Hierarchy & Binding System
#### Meta-Data
- **Type:** World Secret / Public Knowledge (varies by social class)
- **Scope:** Global (Verdant Kingdoms)
- **Status:** Active
- **Time Period:** Timeless (foundational law of the world)
- **Canon Level:** Absolute

#### Knowledge & Awareness
- **Known By:** All citizens, slavers, Pillars, travelers
- **Unknown To:** None (basic fact of life)
- **Discoverable By:** Daily life, observation of collars and auras

#### Affected Parties
- **Directly Affected:** Every enslaved individual, Positive Essence users, Cursed Essence users
- **Indirectly Affected:** Economy, social hierarchy, travel safety

#### Main Narrative / Explanation
Positive (Golden) Essence is the dominant force used for control — it powers golden collars that suppress will, create auras of authority, and bind individuals. Cursed (Crimson) Essence is the counter-force capable of nullifying Positive bindings without visible flare or warning. Ilyas demonstrated a wide unfocused Cursed nullification zone that instantly killed all external Positive Essence in the Gilded Collar Tavern, rendering collars inert and auras useless. This is not common knowledge but was witnessed by an entire tavern.

#### Rules & Constraints
- Positive Essence cannot naturally resist Cursed nullification.
- Golden collars become ordinary metal once their Positive Essence is nullified.
- Forcible application of Beast-Kin traits via Jailbroken Blood Manipulation is possible and creates lasting visual/perception changes (48-hour public perception curse in this case).

#### Current Impact (Today’s Effect)
- Politics: Pillars’ authority can be publicly stripped in seconds.
- Combat: Cursed Essence users like Ilyas have massive advantage against Positive users.
- Powers: Nullification zones create temporary “mundane” pockets where magic fails.
- NPC behavior: Witnesses now fear and spread rumors of the “Violet Phantom.”

### Public Perception & Slave Marking
#### Meta-Data
- **Type:** Public Knowledge
- **Scope:** Regional (Lower Rings)
- **Status:** Active
- **Time Period:** Ongoing
- **Canon Level:** Absolute

#### Knowledge & Awareness
- **Known By:** Everyone in Lower Rings
- **Unknown To:** Upper Kingdom elites (rarely witness)
- **Discoverable By:** Daily life, witnessing a marked individual

#### Affected Parties
- **Directly Affected:** Lord Veyris Thalor (now visually Beast-Kin)
- **Indirectly Affected:** All who interact with him in the next 48 hours

#### Main Narrative / Explanation
Forcible Beast-Kin traits (violet-tipped tail and furred ears) applied via Ilyas’s blood manipulation cause the victim to be perceived as an escaped slave rather than a Pillar. Veyris was stripped of coat, aura, and dignity in front of witnesses; the visual change overrides his previous status.

#### Rules & Constraints
- The curse is temporary (48 hours) but perception shift is immediate and public.
- Reporting the incident accelerates social downfall.

#### Current Impact (Today’s Effect)
- Veyris’s life is effectively destroyed until the curse ends; any attempt to reassert authority will be met with disbelief or exploitation.

## Memories

### The Night the Pillar Fell
#### Meta-Data
- **Memory Type:** Shared / Traumatising
- **Emotional Weight:** High
- **Reliability:** Accurate (multiple witnesses)
- **Time Reference:** Same evening, Gilded Collar Tavern

#### Participants
- **Experienced By:** Ilyas, Lira, Veyris, guards, entire tavern
- **Witnessed By:** All patrons and slaves present
- **Remembered By:** Everyone in the tavern (already spreading as rumor)
- **Forgotten By:** None

#### Perspective & Bias
- **Primary Perspective:** Collective tavern shock mixed with Ilyas’s indifference and Lira’s awe
- **Emotional Bias:** Fear, awe, schadenfreude (toward Veyris)

#### Main Narrative / Recollection
Everyone saw a violet cat become a naked man who nullified magic, broke a collar, stole a Pillar’s coat, and cursed Veyris into a visible Beast-Kin slave for 48 hours. The room went magically dead in seconds.

#### Consequences
- Veyris’s reputation is shattered.
- Lira feels true freedom for the first time.
- Rumors of the “Violet Phantom” now active.

#### Current Impact (Today’s Effect)
- Witnesses will spread the story, making Ilyas a legend and Veyris a target.

#### Meta-Data
- **Memory Type:** Personal / Traumatising → Healing
- **Emotional Weight:** Extreme
- **Reliability:** Accurate (vivid for Lira)
- **Time Reference:** Same night, Ilyas’s kitchen

#### Participants
- **Experienced By:** Lira
- **Witnessed By:** Ilyas
- **Remembered By:** Lira (core memory forming)
- **Forgotten By:** None

#### Perspective & Bias

- **Emotional Bias:** Relief, fear of losing it, budding trust

#### Current Impact (Today’s Effect)
- Lira is now emotionally anchored to Ilyas’s home; will not leave without reason.
