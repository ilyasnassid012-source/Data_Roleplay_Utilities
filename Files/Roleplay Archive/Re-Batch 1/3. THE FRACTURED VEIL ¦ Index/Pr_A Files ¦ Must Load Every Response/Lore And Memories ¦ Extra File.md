# Act I Chapter 1 — Lore & Memories — Chains & Whispers

## Lore

### Essence Hierarchy & Binding System
#### Meta-Data
- **Type:** World Secret / Public Knowledge (varies by social class)
- **Scope:** Global (Verdant Kingdoms)
- **Status:** Active
- **Time Period:** Timeless (foundational law of the world)
- **Canon Level:** Absolute

#### Knowledge & Awareness
- **Known By:** All citizens, slavers, Pillars, travelers
- **Unknown To:** None (basic fact of life)
- **Discoverable By:** Daily life, observation of collars and auras

#### Affected Parties
- **Directly Affected:** Every enslaved individual, Positive Essence users, Cursed Essence users
- **Indirectly Affected:** Economy, social hierarchy, travel safety

#### Main Narrative / Explanation
Positive (Golden) Essence is the dominant force used for control — it powers golden collars that suppress will, create auras of authority, and bind individuals. Cursed (Crimson) Essence is the counter-force capable of nullifying Positive bindings without visible flare or warning. Ilyas demonstrated a wide unfocused Cursed nullification zone that instantly killed all external Positive Essence in the Gilded Collar Tavern, rendering collars inert and auras useless. This is not common knowledge but was witnessed by an entire tavern.

#### Rules & Constraints
- Positive Essence cannot naturally resist Cursed nullification.
- Golden collars become ordinary metal once their Positive Essence is nullified.
- Forcible application of Beast-Kin traits via Jailbroken Blood Manipulation is possible and creates lasting visual/perception changes (48-hour public perception curse in this case).

#### Current Impact (Today’s Effect)
- Politics: Pillars’ authority can be publicly stripped in seconds.
- Combat: Cursed Essence users like Ilyas have massive advantage against Positive users.
- Powers: Nullification zones create temporary “mundane” pockets where magic fails.
- NPC behavior: Witnesses now fear and spread rumors of the “Violet Phantom.”

## Lore

### The Fractured Veil (World Foundation)
#### Meta-Data
- Type: World Secret / Absolute Canon
- Scope: Global
- Status: Active
- Time Period: Timeless (origin unknown, effects ongoing)
- Canon Level: Absolute

#### Knowledge & Awareness
- Known By: All sentient beings in the setting (to varying degrees)
- Unknown To: No one (basic reality everyone lives under)
- Discoverable By: Existing in the world (automatic)

#### Affected Parties
- Directly Affected: Every living creature, society, power structure
- Indirectly Affected: All magic, contracts, slavery system, essence mechanics

#### Main Narrative / Explanation
The Veil is fractured — a permanent metaphysical tear between the mortal realm and other planes. Positive, Negative, and Cursed essences leak through constantly. This enables magic, spirit interactions, curses, and contracts but also fuels systemic oppression (slavery of essence-weak species) and power imbalances.

#### Rules & Constraints
- Essence grades determine power ceiling (B-grade like Ilyas is mid-tier)
- Cover/Release/Inner/Summon mechanics govern usage
- Cursed essences corrupt users over time
- Slavery collars suppress essence & agency (Lira was collared until Ilyas broke it)

#### Current Impact (Today’s Effect)
- Enables Ilyas’s blood manipulation & cat form
- Allows Saori’s dragon-kin traits & raw physicality
- Lira’s fox-kin scent sensitivity & tail/ear expressiveness
- Underpins entire Lower Ring power dynamics & rumors of “Violet Phantom”

#### Meta-Data
- Type: Public Knowledge / Systemic Reality
- Scope: Regional (Verdant Kingdoms Lower Ring)
- Status: Active
- Time Period: Ongoing for centuries
- Canon Level: Absolute (enforced law)

#### Knowledge & Awareness
- Known By: Everyone in Lower Ring
- Unknown To: Isolated outsiders
- Discoverable By: Living in or visiting the area

#### Affected Parties
- Directly Affected: Slaves (Lira was one), owners (Veyris), liberators (Ilyas)
