### Scene 1 — Psychological Breakdown — "Enough Flirting" Stream Reaction

#### Scene Metadata
- **Scene Type:** Psychological Breakdown
- **Primary Participants:** 
  - Aeba — Reactor
  - Mirael — Observer/Support
- **Location:** Aeba's Lair — Safe Haven Zone (softly lit cushion nest)
- **Time Context:** Immediate post-Episode 2 rest phase, concurrent with Ilyas's live stream
- **Duration:** Several minutes of escalating panic
- **POV(s):** Aeba's POV dominant
- **Tone/Atmosphere:** Overwhelming shy panic mixed with hidden delight

#### Pre-Scene Context
Aeba had been quietly watching Ilyas's stream from her lair, developing a quiet crush after seeing him in the Constant Library. The stream was active with Ilyas casually interacting with his orbiting Hamster Key. Viewer metrics were already high from prior episodes. Aeba's Brush-Key hovered nearby, reacting to her emotions.

**Character States Entering:**
- **Aeba:** Physically relaxed in cushions, emotionally flushed and hopeful, fixated on Ilyas's violet hair.
- **Mirael:** Calm and watchful, monitoring Aeba's reactions and filtering comments.

---

#### Full Scene Narration
Aeba leaned closer to the hologram, cheeks already pink as Ilyas spoke casually to his Key. The words "Enough flirting" landed. Aeba's breath caught. Her hands flew to her mouth. The Brush-Key spun worriedly. She hid behind her sketchpad, voice muffled in pure panic. Mirael gently patted her shoulder, trying to calm her while the comment flood exploded with shipping and rizz memes. Aeba's floating blue hair swayed faster. The Brush-Key chimed anxiously. Aeba made small, overwhelmed sounds into the cushion. Mirael filtered negative comments before Aeba could see them. The scene ended with Aeba still hidden but the first strand of emotional impact planted.

##### Opening Beat
Aeba watching stream intently, Brush-Key hovering calmly.

##### Progression — Turn 1
Ilyas says the line on stream. Aeba freezes, eyes wide, then collapses behind sketchpad.

##### Progression — Turn 2
Aeba muffled panic. Brush-Key spins faster. Mirael begins gentle revival attempts.

##### Climax / Peak Moment
Aeba's full shy meltdown, hiding face completely as comments flood with "Violet Guy rizzed her".

##### Resolution / Aftermath
Aeba still hidden in cushions, first wave of panic subsiding but crush deepened. Transition to next stream line.

Aeba eventually laughed through the embarrassment while the Brush-Key spun happily.

- **Primary Participants:** 
  - Ilyas — Finisher

- **Time Context:** Mid-quest after rooftop meeting
- **Duration:** Extended boss fight to finisher
- **POV(s):** Ilyas's POV
- **Tone/Atmosphere:** Effortless cool amid cartoon chaos

#### Pre-Scene Context

**Character States Entering:**
- **Ilyas:** Casual, melon soda in hand, resigned but ready.
- **Hibari:** Excited, ready to distract.
- **Janitor-zombie:** Enraged, mop ready.

#### Full Scene Narration
Ilyas spun the stop sign carelessly then threw it tip-first, piercing the boss's chest. He side-stepped a bleach projectile, rolled under another, then ran while circling opposite Hibari's break-dance spins. He leaned down as the mop swung, crouched to dodge, then used the sign as a trampoline to launch into the air. He sipped mid-air, then dropkicked the boss downward hard onto the embedded sign. The pole pierced deeper. The sign cut the head clean off as Ilyas landed in a crouch, still sipping melon soda in a cool pose.
