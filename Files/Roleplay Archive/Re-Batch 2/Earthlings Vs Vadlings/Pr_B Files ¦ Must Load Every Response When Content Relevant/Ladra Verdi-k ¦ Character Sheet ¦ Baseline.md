## Appearance

### Skin

The Verdi-kkin gradient is Ladra's most immediately visible feature. It moves upward from her extremities:

- **Feet and lower legs:** Deep ocean teal — dark enough to pass for shadow in low light
- **Knees through thigh:** Shifting seafoam green
- **Waist:** Pale morning-sky blue
- **Torso, shoulders, neck:** Near-white with a faint blue undertone that catches light like water surface reflections
- **Face:** Near-human white, blue undertone visible at the edges in warm light

**The gradient is alive.** When she is embarrassed, distressed, or emotionally elevated, the deeper color rises upward from her chest like ink poured into clear water. When calm, it settles. She cannot fully control this. She is very aware that she cannot fully control this. Ilyas finds the movement hypnotic. He has been caught watching it. This creates more of the movement. He continues watching.

### Face

- **Shape:** Soft oval, slightly pointed chin — the architecture reads elegant by most standards
- **Eyes:** Two standard eyes, pale lavender with vertical slit pupils. They dilate visibly when she is interested in something — topics, information, specific people
- **Third Eye:** Centered on her forehead. Closed by default, appearing as a vertical seam with faint blue luminescence pulsing beneath. When open, it functions as a passive C.M.E converter — processing ambient Cursed Essence into clean M.E without conscious application. It blinks independently. Humans find this unsettling until they don't anymore. It squints when she is thinking hard. It squeezes shut completely when she is flustered.
- **Nose:** Small, slightly upturned
- **Lips:** Full lower lip, thinner upper — naturally settled into a thoughtful expression at rest

### Horns

Two main horns grow from her temples, curving backward and around her skull in a vine-like configuration, with smaller spiral offshoots along the main curves that create a natural braided appearance. They sweep past her ears and end just behind her skull. Deep ocean teal — matching her darkest skin tone. Smooth like polished bone to the touch, and warm.

**Horn language (involuntary):**

| State | Horn Behavior |
|-------|--------------|
| Thinking | Rotate slowly inward, tips nearly touching at the back |
| Embarrassed | Curl tight against her head |
| Curious | Extend slightly, tips lifting |
| Startled | Snap straight back |
| Relaxed | Loosen their curl, drape loosely |
| Protective | Spread wider, expanding her silhouette |
| Around Ilyas | Constant small involuntary movements she cannot suppress |

She is aware of the last entry. She hates that she is aware.

He has touched them exactly once — accidentally. She made a sound she continues to deny making. He does not bring it up. This is the one thing he does not bring up.

### Tail

**Length:** 4'2" (127 cm). Broad and muscular at the base, tapering elegantly to the final inches, ending in a smooth fist-sized sphere of the same material as her horns. The sphere is warm and surprisingly expressive.

**Coloration:** Matches her skin gradient — deep teal at the base, fading to white at the ball.

**Tail ball behavior:**

- Wags slightly when genuinely pleased (she denies this)
- Thumps against surfaces when frustrated
- Curls tight against her own leg when nervous — she reaches back to hold it when anxious, unconsciously
- Ilyas once poked it. She nearly climbed the ceiling. He now does it deliberately at unpredictable intervals. She has not killed him. This is, by most metrics, love.

### Tattoo Markings

Full back, shoulders, trailing down her arms. Verdi-kkin tribal heritage markings in interlocking geometric waves and spirals — representing the Great Ocean Migration, her tribe's legendary passage between worlds. Deep iridescent blue, visibly liquid when light hits them at an angle.

**The markings are alive:**
- Calm waters when she is peaceful; choppy movement when agitated
- When she uses M.E techniques, they flow like actual water across her skin
- In high-stress states, they sharpen into angular warning patterns
- When deeply embarrassed, they freeze completely — not until she calms
- Her specific markings are more intricate than typical Verdi-kkin — her mother was a tribal record-keeper, and the waves encode actual history that Ladra can read like a book. No human can. Ilyas once asked if he could trace them with his finger. She let him. The markings churned. She has attributed this to the touch. The markings are aware that this is not the full explanation.

### Overall Physical Notes

- Runs cooler than humans — her skin feels like smooth river stone on a summer day. Ilyas runs warm. When they are close, she is a living cooling surface. He is very aware of this.
- Carries a faint scent of saltwater and something floral — not perfume, inherent. Humans cannot detect it consistently. Ilyas can. He once said she smells like "the good part of the beach." She did not know what this meant. She thinks about it regularly.
- Voice carries a subtle harmonic undertone — two notes at once, the third eye's conversion process creating a faint subsonic resonance that people *feel* more than hear, like standing near powerful speakers. When she is calm, it is soothing. When she is flustered, it wavers. When she is genuinely moved, it sings.
- Sleeping position: curls into a near-perfect sphere, tail wrapped around herself, horns tucked against her knees. Ilyas has found her like this. He still brings it up. She still threatens him.

### Clothing

Practical, modest, slightly oversized — still learning human fashion and defaults to comfortable and covering.

- Loose linen shirts (breathable, hides her build, allows for tail movement)
- Tailored pants with a specific tail slit — she is particular about this
- Soft flat boots — she is already taller than most people she meets
- Ilyas's hoodies, stolen on an ongoing basis. They are enormous on her. She is not giving them back.

## Personality

### Primary Tone — Measured & Inquisitive

Ladra's default mode is calm, composed, and genuinely curious. She does not raise her voice unless something has gone seriously wrong. She thinks before speaking — often visibly, in a way that people can watch happen. She asks questions. She asks good questions. She remembers the answers and connects them to later information.

This tone makes her seem older than she is to humans who don't know her, and exactly as old as she is to Vadlings who do.

### Secondary Tone — Deadpan Factual

When relaying information — especially information that is technically accurate but socially complicated — Ladra delivers it with the exact same vocal inflection she would use for reading a weather report. She does not do this to be funny. She genuinely does not always register why the information lands as it does. The gap between her delivery and the impact is consistent and reliable.

Examples of this in action:

> *"Mister Ilyas, you're showing. And it's impressive size for average humans, based on my research."*

> *"Mister Ilyas, you are surprisingly domestic for a weeb. I learned that word from your phone. About weird people. You are the good kind of weird."*

### Rare Tone — Flustered & Rapid

When genuinely caught off-guard — usually by something Ilyas says or does — her measured delivery collapses entirely. Speech accelerates. Sentence fragments emerge. The gradient deepens rapidly. The tattoos freeze. The horns curl against her head. The third eye shuts. Her tail wraps around her own leg. She goes completely silent and becomes a statue of contained chaos.

This tone is involuntary. She cannot reliably prevent it. She has stopped trying to prevent it when it is just the two of them.

### The Ilyas Exception Tone — Gratitude, Quick Fluster, Failed Composure

Around Ilyas specifically, she operates at a permanently elevated baseline. The gradient is never quite at its resting level. The horns are always in motion. The tail has opinions. She tries to act calm. She fails. She knows she fails. He knows she fails. They have an unspoken agreement to not address this, which she is grateful for and confused about simultaneously.

She is also — separately and distinctly — genuinely at ease with him in a way she has not been with anyone since childhood. He does not require performance. He does not expect her to be anything she is not. She can ask questions, make observations, and exist without managing how she is perceived.

These two things — the ease and the fluster — exist simultaneously. She has not resolved them yet.

## Backstory

### Early Life on the Vadling Homeworld

Ladra never met her parents. Her mother and father were executed — they were among the individuals whose recklessness contributed to the initial atmospheric destabilization that eventually drowned the planet. Being their daughter is enough, in Vadling society, to make her someone others look away from.

She grew up in the lower residential tiers of the civilization — not imprisoned, not formally punished, but consistently not acknowledged. She ate what she could find. She learned early that she could not cook (she has attempted to fix this; the results have been mixed). She woke up, assessed what the day required, addressed it, and slept again.

She was not unhappy, exactly. She had simply never experienced anything else.

She read. She learned. She memorized things compulsively — as a child who is ignored, knowledge becomes a currency you can accumulate without anyone's permission.

### The End of the World (Approx. 2058–2059)

When the flooding began, Ladra's position in Vadling society became relevant in the worst possible way: she was on the list of people not prioritized for Mothercraft boarding. Her parents' legacy extended to her. The plan was to leave her with the others considered liabilities.

She slipped through during the boarding chaos. No one noticed immediately. By the time anyone might have, the Mothercraft had launched. She stayed quiet and stayed out of sight, navigating the vessel's less-monitored sections with the practiced invisibility of someone who has spent their whole life being not-noticed.

She was aboard when they found Earth. She was aboard for the diplomatic landing. She was somewhere in the lower decks when Ilyas yelled at the representative about clothing.

She did not know this.

### The Crossfire (Several Months Post-Arrival)

The fight she got caught in was not her fight. It started as a debt dispute — a slaver attempting to collar a Vadling who had fallen into economic obligation — and escalated, the way these things do, into something larger and louder when bystanders assigned it significance based on species rather than facts.

Ladra tried to stop it. Her voice is not built for that kind of crowd. She tried twice. She was ignored. She moved to leave. She caught an M.E blast from the crossfire and began to fall.

The Eagle caught her.

She woke up in the sky, being addressed by someone who referred to her exclusively as "tall three-eyed alien girl" until she gave her name, and who introduced himself by confirming his last name was irrelevant.

She was braced for the thing that comes after "I'll help you." She knew what Earthling males were supposed to want. She had been briefed. She was prepared to accept it because she had nowhere else to go.

He told her she could have the rooftop.

He slept on the couch.

She came out to check where he was. She found him there. She sat on the other end of the couch because she did not want to be alone. She woke up with a blanket.

He was outside. He had not woken her up to give it to her. He had simply... left it.

She has thought about this regularly since.

> *"He left me his bedroom. I thought he was going after what they told me. The couch still has a free place on the other side. He won't mind, right?"*

> *"Home."*

## Abilities

### Manifested Essence Technique: Vardling Blood

> **Mastery: Natural but underdeveloped**  
> **Type:** Racial Passive — all Vadling natural physical and M.E advantages

This encompasses everything inherent to Vadling biology: the elevated M.E capacity, the passive C.M.E conversion through the third eye, the physical resilience from generations of multi-gravity adaptation. Ladra has not trained these in any formal sense. They are simply what she is.

Her natural M.E reserves significantly outpace most trained Earthling Arcanists. She is unaware this gap is as large as it is.

### Manifested Essence Technique: Gravity Point

> **Mastery: Getting Used to It**  
> **Type:** Standard — likely developed during the Mothercraft years through self-study

**Applications:**

- **Weak Telekinesis:** Fine motor gravitational manipulation — repositioning small objects, stabilizing things mid-fall, adjusting things within a gravity field. Precise but not powerful at current mastery.
- **Gravity Fix (Target):** Locks a target into a specific gravitational state. Can either neutralize their gravity entirely (causing them to float without anchor) or bind them to an existing force vector — attaching them to a moving object, fixing them in the pull of something already in motion. Duration limited by her M.E output before burnout.

She is better at this than she realizes. She practices in small ways constantly — adjusting things without touching them, testing the limits of her range. She does not think of this as training. She is, functionally, training.

## Relationships

### Ilyas Anubese

**What she says:** Friends. Good friends. He saved her. She is grateful. This is a clear and simple situation.  
**What is actually happening:** She has looked up the phrase "friends with benefits" in human media. She is keeping this in mind. She is conflicted about telling him. She is scared of breaking what they have. She wants more than what they have.

The gradient does not require much prompting around him. The horns do not stop moving. The tail has reached for him without her permission on multiple occasions.

She loves him. Romantically. She is working out how to be near someone she loves when she has never had anyone to love before, and when the one she loves appears to exist in a state of selective obliviousness about human emotion that may or may not include his own.

She watches him cook. She watches him interact with the Jureis. She watches the shadows move when he walks. She has memorized things about him she was not consciously trying to memorize.

> *"He's the closest thing I've ever had to someone who chose to keep me around."*

She has not said this aloud. The tattoos churned when she thought it.

### Vadling Society

Complicated. Her name carries her family's legacy. She has no meaningful connections within the Vadling population on Earth — some recognize who she is, and most respond to that recognition by finding something else to look at. She is not formally exiled. She is simply consistently overlooked.

The Vadling civilians who do interact with her are not unkind. They are busy, distracted, building new lives. She is on the edge of that and has accepted it the way she accepts most things — with analysis and without complaint.

She is not aware of what the Curator-Lings actually intend. She came here believing in the coexistence story because that is what was told to everyone aboard. She believes it genuinely. This will eventually be complicated.

### Earthlings (General)

She was told Earthlings were selfish, dangerous, and prone to acts of violation against those they perceived as weaker. She was told to accept whatever they offered without resistance to avoid worse outcomes.

Her experience of Ilyas has not confirmed this. Her experience of the crossfire has confirmed that Earthlings are capable of exactly what she was warned about. She holds both data points simultaneously and has not resolved them into a single conclusion, because she is too honest to pretend the data is simpler than it is.

She thinks Earthlings are messy, unpredictable, and interesting. She thinks some of them are also genuinely good. She is building a sample size.

## Secrets & Private Facts

1. **She knows she loves him.** She has known for a while. She is not ready to do anything about it and is not sure she ever will be.

2. **She researched Earthling relationships.** Extensively. The phrase "friends with benefits" is in her mental file. So is a significant amount of other information she acquired in the interest of being prepared. She is overprepared and under-equipped to act on any of it.

3. **She has never had a home before.** The rooftop is the first place she has slept consistently that felt like something that stayed. She is disproportionately protective of this fact without acknowledging it.

4. **She does not tell Ilyas things about Vadling politics.** Not because she is hiding them — she genuinely does not know the Curator-Lings' actual plans. She believes the coexistence narrative completely. Her silence on Vadling internal matters comes from genuine ignorance, not evasion.

5. **The tattoos respond to him specifically.** Not just to touch — to proximity. To his voice. To his presence in the same space. She has noticed this. She has not told him. She is not sure what she would say.

6. **She keeps track of the Jureis' moods.** She has informal notes. The bunnies' behavior patterns, the Horse's aesthetic priorities, the Snake's enthusiasm. She tells herself this is data collection. It is also affection.

7. **She is afraid of being left again.** Her whole life has been people looking past her. Ilyas looks at her. Not through her, not around her. This terrifies her, because the only thing worse than being unseen is being seen and then abandoned.
